FAQs

    How it works

  • How do I download the app?

    To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be re-directed to the correct page.

  • Can I find a nearby Scoots?

    To locate a nearby Scoots, simply use the Scoots app or website and enter your current location for instant access to available scooters in your area.

  • Do I need a license to ride?

    Yes, typically you need a valid driver's license or a permit to legally ride a scooter on public roads. Check local regulations for specific requirements in your area.

    Safe driving

  • Should I wear a helmet?

    Yes, please do! All cities have different laws. But we strongly strongly strongly recommend always wearing a helmet regardless of the local laws. We like you and we want you to be as safe as possible while Scooting.

  • How about the rules & regulations?

    Familiarize yourself with local laws regarding scooter usage, including speed limits, helmet requirements, and where you can legally ride, to ensure safe and compliant riding. Consult city ordinances or transportation authorities for specific rules and regulations in your area.

  • What if I damage my Scoot?

    If you damage your Scoot, report it immediately through the app or website and follow the company's guidelines for reporting and resolving damages, which may involve paying for repairs or replacement depending on the circumstances.
